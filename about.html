About BrainBench
Know your limits. Push them.

What is BrainBench?

BrainBench is a platform designed to test and improve your cognitive skills. From reaction time to memory challenges, we provide interactive tools that help you understand how your brain performs under pressure.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make brain training fun, accessible, and competitive. We believe everyone can improve their mental performance with consistent practice.

Features

  • ⚡ Reaction Time Tests
  • ⌨️ Typing Speed Challenges
  • 🎯 Aim Trainer
  • 🧠 Memory & Sequence Tests
  • 📊 Performance Tracking Dashboard
